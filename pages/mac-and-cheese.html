Macaroni and cheese

Description

Macaroni and cheese is a gooey, all-American favorite that can be baked in the oven for a crunchy finish.

Ingredients

  • 16 oz elbow macaroni
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 6 tbsp butter
  • 1/3 cup flour
  • 3 cups whole milk
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 4 cups sharp cheddar cheese
  • 2 cups Gruyere cheese
  • 1 cup panko breadcrumbs
  • Salt
  • Pepper

Steps

  1. Heat water in a large pot until boiling, then add macaroni and cook until al dente.
  2. Drain the cooked macaroni and add olive oil to coat pasta.
  3. Melt butter in a saucepan over medium heatand whisk in flour until cooked.
  4. Whisk in the milk and heavy cream until smooth.
  5. Add salt and pepper to taste.
  6. Add two cups each of the cheddar cheese and Gruyere cheese, then stir until melted.
  7. Add in cooked past and mix until smooth.
  8. Pour the macaroni and cheese into an oven-safe baking dish and cover with the rest of the cheese and panko breadcrumbs.
  9. Bake in the oven at 350F for 30 minutes until golden brown and crunchy.
