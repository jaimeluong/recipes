Mashed potatoes

Description

Mashed potatoes are one of my favorite Thanksgiving side dishes because they are delicious and full of flavor.

Ingredients

  • 5 pounds of potatoes
  • 2 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 6 tbsp of butter
  • 1 cup of whole milk
  • 4 oz cream cheese
  • Green onions
  • Salt
  • Pepper

Steps

  1. Dice mashed potatoes into inch-sized chunks and add them to a large pot of cold water.
  2. Add salt into the water and turn the heat to high to boil the water.
  3. Cook the potatoes for 10 to 12 minutes until soft.
  4. Use a potato masher to mash the potatoes up, then add milk, garlic, butter, and cream cheese.
  5. Stir the mashed potatoes until smooth, then add salt and pepper to taste.
  6. Add salt and pepper to taste before serving.
